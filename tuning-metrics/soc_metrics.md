# SOC Metrics (Portfolio Template)

## Suggested metrics to track
- Alert volume by category (auth/email/endpoint/cloud/dlp)
- True positive vs false positive rate
- Mean time to acknowledge (MTTA)
- Mean time to resolve (MTTR)
- Top noisy detections (for tuning)

## Example (format)
| Week | Alerts Reviewed | TP | FP | Escalations | Notes |
|------|-----------------|----|----|-------------|------|
| 2026-W05 | 350 | 12 | 338 | 3 | Major phishing campaign increased email alerts |
